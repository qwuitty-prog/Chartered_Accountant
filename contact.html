Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly can I expect a response to my inquiry?

We strive to respond to all inquiries within 24 hours during business days. For urgent matters, you can call us directly at +91 9004329806.

Do you offer remote consultation services?

Yes, we offer comprehensive remote consultation services via video calls, phone calls, and email. We can handle most accounting and tax matters remotely.

What documents should I prepare for my first consultation?

For your first consultation, please bring your PAN card, Aadhaar card, business registration documents (if applicable), previous financial statements, and any relevant tax documents.

Do you handle GST registration and filing?

Yes, we offer complete GST services including registration, monthly/quarterly returns filing, reconciliation, and compliance advisory for businesses of all sizes.

What are your consultation fees?

We offer an initial free 30-minute consultation to understand your needs. After that, our fees depend on the complexity and scope of services required. We provide transparent pricing with no hidden charges.
